# Track Reference — Stat Priorities by Series

Each track has a **1st Stat** (primary priority) and **2nd Stat** (secondary priority). Build your setup — components, drivers, and boosts — to maximize these stats in that order.

These are not numerical weights. They are ranked priorities: always satisfy the 1st stat first, then the 2nd.

## Quick Stat Reference — Which Tracks Demand What

**Overtaking-heavy series/tracks:** Sao Paulo, Hungaroring, Miami, Las Vegas, Spa, Abu Dhabi
**Defending-heavy:** Monaco, Monza, Jeddah, Shanghai, Zandvoort
**Tyre Management-heavy:** Barcelona, Sakhir, Suzuka, Silverstone (S3+), Baku, Mexico
**Race Start-heavy:** Melbourne, Spielberg, Austin (S7), Canada, Singapore
**Power Unit secondary:** Sao Paulo, Sakhir, Baku, Mexico, Canada, Spa, Shanghai
**Speed secondary:** Silverstone, Miami, Monza, Jeddah, Las Vegas, Abu Dhabi, Singapore
**Cornering secondary:** Melbourne, Hungaroring, Monaco, Suzuka, Zandvoort, Austin (S7/S10)
