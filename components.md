- **SPD** – Speed | **CRN** – Cornering | **PWR** – Power Unit | **QUA** – Qualifying

- **OVT** – Overtake Modifier | **IMP** – Impact | **DUR** – Duration | **RCH** – Recharge *(Battery only)*
- **RAR** – Rarity | **SRS** – Series this component is designed for

## Component Selection Logic

When recommending a setup for a track, match components to track stat priorities:

- **Speed-priority tracks** → favor high SPD: Loose Fit (FW), Pressure Point (Brakes), Bounce (Suspension), Downforce (RW), Lockdown (GB), Velocity (Engine)
- **Cornering-priority tracks** → favor high CRN: Laminar/Scythe (FW), Stabilix (Brakes), Curver 3.0/Motion Link V2 (Suspension), Impact (RW), Fracture/Shift X (GB), Overdrive/Tempest (Engine)
- **Power Unit-priority tracks** → favor high PWR: Leading Edge (FW), Aura Lock (Brakes), Equilibrium (Suspension), Dominus (RW), Powerbox (GB), Hotfix/Axis 3000 (Engine)
- **Qualifying-priority tracks** → favor high QUA: Scythe (FW), Scramble (Brakes), Stratos (GB), DriveOS (Engine)
- **Pit Time-priority** → favor lowest PIT values across all slots
- **Overtake-heavy tracks** → prioritize Battery OVT + IMP; Spare Pack and Charge Core lead here

A higher-level weaker component can outperform a lower-level stronger one — always use actual current stats at the player's reported level.
